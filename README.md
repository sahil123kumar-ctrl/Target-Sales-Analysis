# Target Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes **Target Corporation’s sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency** using **SQL** for data processing and **Power BI** for visualization.  
The goal is to extract meaningful business insights, track performance trends, and recommend strategies to improve sales and customer satisfaction.

---

## About Target Corporation
**Target** is one of the largest retail companies in the United States, offering products across categories such as apparel, electronics, groceries, home essentials, and beauty items.  
The company focuses on **quality, affordability, and omnichannel convenience**, operating through both physical stores and online platforms.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset simulates Target’s **e-commerce operations between September 2016 and October 2018**, covering customers, products, transactions, and reviews.

| Table Name      | Description                                             |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Customers**    | Customer IDs, city, and state information               |
| **Geolocation**  | Location mapping for addresses                          |
| **Orders**       | Order timestamps, statuses, and delivery details        |
| **Order Items**  | Product-level details (price, freight value, seller ID) |
| **Payments**     | Payment types, amounts, and installment data            |
| **Products**     | Product category, weight, and attributes                |
| **Sellers**      | Seller details and geographic information               |
| **Reviews**      | Customer review scores and written feedback             |

## Conclusion

| Area                       | Objective                 | Key Insights                                                                                       |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Sales Growth**            | Track revenue trends      | Revenue increased steadily, peaking in **November 2017** due to **Black Friday** and holiday sales. |
| **Top Categories**          | Identify best performers  | **Health & Beauty** and **Bed Table Bath** were the highest revenue-generating categories.          |
| **Geography**               | Find high-value regions   | **São Paulo (SP)** contributed the most customers and revenue; expansion in other states is needed. |
| **Delivery & Fulfillment**  | Evaluate logistics        | **97% fulfillment rate**, though **RR** and **ES** states showed longer delivery times.             |
| **Customer Satisfaction**   | Review customer feedback  | Overall rating between **4–5 stars**, with lower scores in **Insurance & Services** category.       |
| **Payment Analysis**        | Assess payment patterns   | **Credit cards** were most used; some mismatches found in total vs. expected payment values.        |
| **Seller Insights**         | Evaluate seller activity  | Top sellers performed well; others had **high cancellation rates**, impacting reliability.          |

---

## Recommendations to Boost Sales
1. **Leverage Seasonal Peaks** – Plan major **Black Friday and holiday promotions** with bundles, discounts, and free shipping.  
2. **Expand Beyond São Paulo (SP)** – Target emerging states with localized marketing campaigns and faster deliveries.  
3. **Enhance Logistics** – Optimize delivery routes and offer **SLA-based incentives** to sellers in delayed regions.  
4. **Strengthen Customer Retention** – Introduce **loyalty programs** and **personalized recommendations** for repeat buyers.  
5. **Boost Product Visibility** – Highlight high-rated but underperforming items through featured listings or advertisements.  
6. **Improve Low-Rated Categories** – Investigate issues in **Insurance & Services** and enhance service quality.  
7. **Ensure Payment Accuracy** – Automate reconciliation processes to eliminate mismatched payment records.  
8. **Support Sellers** – Offer training to underperformers and provide rewards for consistent top performers.  
9. **Optimize Promotion Timing** – Conduct **night-time flash sales**, when order volumes typically peak.
